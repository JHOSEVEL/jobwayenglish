Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform

Our AI-powered platform matches your skills, experience, and preferences with suitable Canadian job openings. Employers receive ranked candidates based on job requirements, and you receive personalized job recommendations.

We specialize in tech, engineering, healthcare, and skilled trades roles that are in high demand in Canada. Most positions require at least 2 years of professional experience and often offer work visa sponsorship.

The hiring process typically takes 2-4 months from initial contact to job offer. Visa processing times vary but expect 2-6 months depending on the type of work permit. We provide guidance throughout the entire process to keep things moving smoothly.

No, creating a profile and accessing job opportunities is completely free for professionals. Employers pay placement fees only after successful hires. You're responsible for standard government visa fees and relocation costs.

We offer comprehensive post-arrival support including help with housing, banking, healthcare registration, and cultural orientation. Our team connects you with Brazilian communities in your new city and remains available for questions during your transition.
